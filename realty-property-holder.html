Realty Property Holder

Online rental market space

I am too familiar with the struggle of find and affordable a decent place to live. The competitiveness of trying to find a place is one of the most stressful factors. Realty Property Holder is to help renters to find homes that they love by making the apartment hunting process as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

USER GOAL

To find place to live that meet their needs

BUSINESS GOAL

Positioning themselves clearly in the market, can attract tenants who are looking for a specific type of property, minimize inconvenience for tenants and lanlords, providing other professionals, such as electricians, plumbers, etc through third-party providers.

DESIGN PROCESS


RESEARCH

I researched the company and the rental marketplace industry in order to develop a more informed perspective, learn the language of the space, and understand the company’s mission. Focus of this platform is to make this platform a full property management platform which manages properties, tenants, helps finding a tenants, employee management within property management firm, service & utilities management.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Localisation
As its going to be global covering major countries with all language support (rent.com has this)

Payment Option
integrating the payment options which pays the rent to the landlord or property manager (Hotpads.com has this)

Peer to Peer messaging
two individuals interact directly with each other, without intermediation by a third-party

by this Realty Property Holder will have a single platform for global use with all tools required in an end-to-end flow of searching, managing a rental property by landlord/property management company/property manager.
